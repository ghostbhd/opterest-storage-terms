Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our storage services

How much does student storage cost?

expand_more

Our storage plans start from just $29.99 per month for small items. We offer different storage sizes to accommodate your needs, from small boxes to full apartment storage. View our pricing page for detailed information.

When do you pick up and deliver items?

expand_more

We offer flexible pickup and delivery times, 7 days a week. When you book storage, you'll be able to select a time slot that works best for you. We can also accommodate special requests with advance notice.

How secure is your storage facility?

expand_more

Our storage facilities feature 24/7 surveillance, alarm systems, climate control, and fire protection. Your items are also covered by our insurance while in storage for complete peace of mind.

What if I need my items before the scheduled delivery date?

expand_more

No problem! You can request early access or delivery of your items with a 48-hour notice. There may be a small fee for expedited service, but we're always happy to accommodate your needs.
